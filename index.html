Key takeaway
The piece argues that the agent boom is producing wildly different categories of "agent" — from prompt-wrapped chatbots to genuinely autonomous multi-step systems — and most PM agent backlogs conflate them, leading to over- or under-investment. The framework PMs should adopt before writing a single agent PRD: classify the agent by autonomy level (assisted, supervised, autonomous), surface (chat, sidebar, background), and trust profile (read-only, write-with-confirmation, fully empowered) — then size investment to category, not to "agent" as a monolith.
